About me

I'm a fullstack developer with experience in freelance. My intention is to get django developers more power in their search of a fulltime job, freelance gig or a part-time contract

About project

Django is one of the best and most popular fullstack frameworks in the world. Famous companies such as Youtube, Spotify, Instagram use django. And Django devs is here to give BACK the power to the developers.


Freelance work is a rollercoaster. There are highs and lows, somedays you get dozen requests and the other days you struggle to find work.


Job boards are for the full-time folks. Freelancers and contractors rarely find a job on the job boards. So with this intention we have created Django devs.


Let companies reach out to you and get leads more easily.


This project was partly insipired by railsdevs.com
